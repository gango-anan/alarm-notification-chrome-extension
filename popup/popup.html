20-20-20 Rule

Take a 20-second break every 20 minutes to look at an object 20 feet away.

Learn more about eye exercises

Eye Exercises

Blinking: Blinking frequently can help to lubricate your eyes and reduce dryness caused by staring at a screen.

Eye massage: Gently massage your eyelids in circular motions with your fingertips to reduce tension around the eyes.

Focus change: Look at an object close to you, such as your hand or a pen, and then shift your gaze to an object far away, such as a tree or a building.
